There are four adolescents who come from around to limit the parcels that must be grown by the age group.

After that, it’s the age group that come now to cultivate the fields.

This is what we call in Malinke: Kassa. Kassa, remove the herbs.

Afterwards, it is the drummers who come to encourage the workers in the field.

After the age group, it is the owner of the field who now comes to give a goat to the age group who are doing Kassa, as their source.

We kill this beast, we take the meat to the village to prepare.

And after that, it’s the girls who come with breakfast.

And every girl was chosen by their friends. Among all the young, each has his friend among the young girls.

And work continues! it continues…

There are times when you can use other rhythms in a group called Kassa. What rhythms are used? The first rhythm is the Soro.

After Soro, we use Doi Foli ??

After Doi Foli ??, we use the Kon - the daddy and the mama of all Malinké rhythms. Sulfur of rhythms, the base of all Malinké rhythms.

Come on! Even the Soni, we play in moments of Kassa.

All that, it comes from Soro. This is the name of the gri-gri fetish. It’s a gri-gri which is very strong … very strong!

That you can even tie all your colleagues to win the girl who was chosen as the princess. It is he who finishes his plot, he will win this girl. It’s Soro who does that.

What do we see now? What is happening? It is the arrival of the great eating that is called Kondon. After the Kondon, the arrival of Kondon, one plays other rhythms in the Kassa. And this rhythm is called Kondon ?? Dunun.

We begin work, what rhythm that still comes, which makes among the Kassa? It’s Dibon.

After Dibon, the work is finished, there is another rhythm that is used called N’Donna So.

After N’Donna So, we now prove the villagers, the people, that the work that advised us, we did the work, it was done.

This rhythm is called N’ko ké.

After N’ko ké, there are still three folios.

And the three foli, it goes on, it goes on, everyone comes to show his bravery what he did in the fields, and it’s over, and a little Bada.

So, what do we say Kassa, it is the Soro that is at the base of Kassa. In the place of Kassa, it is the Soro which is the first rhythm that is used. So, here is the Kassa.
